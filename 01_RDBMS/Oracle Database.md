## Oracle Database Release Types

- Long Term Release (LTS)
	- 5 years of Premier support followed by 3 years of Extended Support
	- Recommended to production
	- 26ai is the latest LTS
- Innovation Release (IR)
	- 2 years of Premier Support, no Extended Support
	- Recommended to explore the new features
	- 12c, 18c and 21c
